Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to gather structured and meaningful feedback from clients of John Malek Law Group (JMLG). As part of an internship project, the goal is to collect input on client satisfaction, communication, and overall experience to help JMLG improve its services and refine company analytics. By understanding client needs better, the company aims to optimize its operations and enhance client relationships.

Audience

The intended audience includes current and past clients of JMLG who are willing to provide feedback on their experiences with the firm's legal services. These individuals will be encouraged to share honest opinions to help improve the firm's quality of service.

Dynamic elements

The site will include a feedback form powered by JavaScript for dynamic interaction. Key functionalities include:

  • Input validation for rating fields (scale of 1 to 10) and text feedback.
  • Display success/error messages upon submission.
  • Pull mock data given, then take the averages of the respnses uses const functions, such as averages and sums
  • Interactive design for better user experience, ensuring responsiveness for mobile and desktop users.

Content

Home page

The home page will introduce the purpose of the feedback platform and explain how clients can contribute to improving JMLG’s services. It will also include an overview of the firm’s values and a call-to-action button linking to the feedback page.

Feedback Page

The feedback page will feature an interactive form where clients can answer the following questions:

  • How would you rate your overall experience with JMLG? (Scale 1-10)
  • How would you rate communication during your case? (Scale 1-10)
  • Are you satisfied with the outcome of your case? (Yes/No)
  • Would you recommend JMLG to others? (Yes/No)
  • Please leave any additional feedback or suggestions for improvement. (Text Area)

JavaScript will validate these inputs and provide visual feedback for required fields. Upon submission, users will see a confirmation message. The data collected will be stored for review and analysis, helping the firm refine its operations.
